ABOUT THE EVENT

IOT has been providing some really great solutions to the day to day life problems. It requires some creative and innovative brains to think and implement such solutions. The objective of this event is to bring out those brains among students. The Hackathon will give them a chance to build IoT based innovate products/systems using the Design Thinking process. As part of this 2-day hackathon, students will identify and solve problems using IOT.

Benefits of students

The participants will be able to create a project on IOT.
To give the participants an idea about IOT.
Students will get exposure, experience and confidence to present their skills on a national level.
To prepare students to work on projects with guidance.
To identify and appreciate students with skills and an innovative mind.
Students will get a chance to interact with the representative of various leading MNCs.
Students would have to prepare a prototype showcasing their ideas.
Students will have the opportunity to grasp internship offers, seed fundings from various MNCs and offer of 10 Day bootcamp at Nasscom.
Students will get to know how to handle real-world scenarios.
Students will learn about various new technologies.
